AutoEncoder for Anomaly Detection

AutoEncoders are often used for denoising images, or highly efficient lossy compression. Did you know they can also be used to detect anomalies that don't conform to a set of training images? I explore using auto-encoders for anomaly detection in manufacturing applications.

Ray Tracer

Ever wonder how images are rendered in movies and video games? This ray tracer (written in C++) does just that, exploring how to write optimized code to mimic the physics underlying how light bounces through a scene, eventually reaching your eye/camera.
